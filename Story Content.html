Overview

 

 

 

Your family has been framed for the murder of the Elevn King. You were captured by the rogue elven soldiers. The soldiers have thrown you in the dungeon along with other humans and criminals. While Sitting in the dungeon, a stranger throws a peice of rolled up paper secretly toward you. You read the note, it's your father last words which describe how to get proof that can make your family inncoent. The child realizes he has to leave this dungeon to get the evidence and reclaim his family honor.
